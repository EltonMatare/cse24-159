Services

Here’s what I do and how I can contribute to your next project.

Film & Character Work

I bring characters to life with intensity, depth, and honesty. Whether it's a quiet warrior or a reluctant savior, I commit fully to every role—because every story deserves truth

Voice Acting & Narration

From animated films to immersive storytelling, I lend my voice to projects that need presence, warmth, and subtle power. Sometimes, less really is more.

Creative Collaboration

I work with musicians, writers, and filmmakers who want to create with heart. Whether it's producing, consulting, or simply vibing over ideas—I believe in building things that mean something.

Public Speaking

I’m not the loudest voice in the room—but I believe in speaking with intention. Whether it’s a panel, a talk, or an intimate Q&A, I share experiences from my journey through film, loss, resilience, and creativity. If the conversation is honest and human, I’m honored to be part of it.

Recent Projects

Project 1

Keanu is Legend

I’ve never chased the spotlight—just tried to stay true to myself. This project reflects the journey I’ve taken, the roles I’ve played, and the quiet moments that define me. I believe strength comes from kindness, and purpose from presence. If this work says anything, I hope it’s that being human matters more than being famous.!

Project 2

The Matrix Mind

The Matrix was more than a film—it was a question, a challenge, a revelation. Playing Neo changed my life. This project dives into those ideas: reality, control, and the courage to choose your own path. I didn’t just wear the glasses—I carried the questions with me. Maybe you will too.

Project 3

John Wick Vengeance

John was built on loss and love. I connected with that. This project captures the precision, pain, and purpose behind the man. It’s not just about action—it’s about what drives us, what we fight for when everything’s been taken. Through every scene, I tried to bring honesty to the violence. A kind of stillness within the storm.

Project 4

Humanity in Motion

I ride motorcycles. I write. I try to listen more than I speak. This project is about all the things that ground me outside of film—the human stuff. Life isn’t always loud, but it’s always moving. These are pieces of my world off-screen, shared with whoever wants to slow down for a moment and feel something real.

Project 5

Constantine

I’ve walked through hell—literally—and I carry the scars every day. Suicide doesn’t end the pain... it stains the soul. I know where I’m headed when it’s over, and it’s not paradise. So I fight. I chain-smoke prayers and exorcise demons, not to be a hero—but to buy back a little grace. Maybe if I save enough souls, I’ll earn a shot at redemption. Until then, I walk the line… between the damned and the divine.

About me

Actor, Musician, Producer

I was born in Beirut, raised in Toronto, and found my place somewhere between action scenes and quiet moments. Over the years, I’ve had the honor of stepping into the shoes of rebels, assassins, lovers, and legends—from Neo to John Wick to Constantine. But the roles never defined me. They were just stories I was lucky enough to tell. Off-screen, I ride motorcycles, write poetry, and try to stay present. I believe in kindness. I believe in working hard, listening more than speaking, and giving people space to be who they are. Life’s not always easy—it’s full of loss, love, silence, and motion—but if I’ve learned anything, it’s that you don’t have to be loud to be strong. Thanks for being here. I hope something in my work makes you feel less alone.
